Our History

Founded in 1990, our rafting company has been providing unforgettable adventures for over three decades. We started with a small team of passionate rafters and have grown into one of the most trusted names in the industry. Our commitment to safety, fun, and environmental stewardship has earned us a loyal following of adventure seekers from around the world.

Over the years, we've expanded our offerings to include a variety of trips, from half-day excursions to multi-day adventures. Whether you're looking for a leisurely float down a scenic river or the adrenaline rush of navigating Class V rapids, we have something for everyone.

Our guides are experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing an exceptional experience for each and every guest. Join us and become part of our storied history as we continue to explore new rivers and create new memories.
